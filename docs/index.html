Your budgets and loans, organized on your phone

CwBudgetTracker helps you plan spending, track recurring budgets, manage loans and payments, view history, and export or back up your data — including optional sync and cloud backup features you control.

Get it on Google Play Privacy policy

What you can do

  • Budgets — Create and edit budgets by period, category, and line items; track planned vs spent; filter, sort, and group; handle recurring budgets with sensible edit options.
  • Loans — Record money you lend or borrow, log payments and issuances, filter and group loans, and view loan history.
  • Statements — Generate loan statements (HTML/PDF) for printing or sharing; optional upload of generated PDFs to secure cloud storage when you choose that action.
  • Calculator — Quick calculations from within the app.
  • Appearance & language — Light, dark, and system themes; multiple languages for the interface; currency preferences.
  • Local backup & restore — Create backup files on your device and restore from a file you select.
  • Google Drive (optional) — Sign in with Google to upload or restore an app backup in your account’s private app-data area on Google Drive (only this app can access that folder).
  • Online sync — When your device is online, budget and loan data can sync with a cloud database so your information stays aligned across sessions (see the Privacy Policy for details).

Trust & policies

  • Privacy policy — what data is stored on your device, what may be processed by Google and Firebase when you use optional features, and your choices.
  • Terms of service — rules for using the app and important limitations (including that the app is not professional financial or legal advice).
