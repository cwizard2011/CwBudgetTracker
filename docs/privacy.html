Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the mobile application CwBudgetTracker (“the App”) handles information when you use it. By using the App, you agree to this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

Who we are

The App is provided by the developer / entity publishing it on the app store Peter Adeoye (Cwizard). For privacy requests, contact: cwizard2011@gmail.com.

Summary

CwBudgetTracker stores your budget and loan information on your device by default. If you are online, the App may synchronize budget and loan data with Google Firebase (Firestore). If you use loan statement features that upload a PDF, those files may be stored in Firebase Storage and metadata in Firestore. If you use Google Drive backup, you sign in with Google and the App stores a backup file in Google Drive’s hidden app data folder for this App only. The App does not sell your personal data and does not include third-party advertising analytics in the application code described here.

Information the App processes

  • Budget data — Titles, amounts, categories, dates, recurrence settings, notes, and related fields you enter.
  • Loan data — Loan amounts, counterpart names, payments, issuances, balances, dates, and notes you enter.
  • Settings — Theme, language/locale, and currency preferences stored locally.
  • Backup files — When you create a local backup, the App writes a structured file (for example JSON) containing the data needed to restore budgets and loans.
  • Google account (Drive only) — If you use Google Drive backup, Google Sign-In is used so the App can access only the App’s private Drive app-data scope; the App does not read your general Google Drive files.
  • Invoice / statement PDFs (optional) — If you use features that generate a loan statement and upload it, a PDF may be stored in Firebase Storage and a small record (e.g. identifier, loan reference, download URL, timestamp) in Firestore.

Local storage on your device

The App keeps budgets, loans, counterparties, queued changes, and settings on the device using the device’s local storage mechanisms (for example Android/iOS secure app storage via the React Native Async Storage layer). This data remains on the device unless you use backup, sync, or upload features described below.

Network, sync, and Firebase (Google)

The App uses the network permission to connect to the internet. When the device is online, the App may:

  • Sync budgets and loans with Google Firebase Firestore — pending changes can be sent to Firestore, and the App may retrieve documents from Firestore collections used for budgets and loans so data can stay consistent when you use the App across devices or sessions. Exact behavior depends on your connectivity and app state.
  • Store invoice PDFs in Firebase Storage and related metadata in Firestore when you explicitly use upload/save features for loan statements.

Firebase services are operated by Google. Google’s privacy policy applies to their processing: https://policies.google.com/privacy. Firebase configuration (project, security rules) is controlled by the App developer; you should use the App only if you trust how the backend is configured.

Google Sign-In and Google Drive (optional)

If you enable Google Drive backup or restore, the App uses Google Sign-In with access limited to the Drive app data scope so backup files can be written to or read from the App’s private area in your Google account. The App does not access your other Drive files. Google’s terms and privacy policy apply to authentication and Drive: Google Privacy Policy.

Local file backup and restore

When you create a backup file on your device or choose a file to restore, that file is under your control. The App does not upload that file except when you separately use Google Drive backup or other cloud features you trigger.

Data retention and deletion

On device: Data remains until you delete it in the App, uninstall the App, or clear app data (depending on your OS). On Firebase: Retention depends on the developer’s Firebase project settings and your use of sync/invoice upload; contact the developer to request deletion of cloud-held data where applicable. On Google Drive: You can disconnect access or delete app data from your Google account according to Google’s tools and policies.

Security

The App uses industry-standard mobile platform protections. No method of storage or transmission is perfectly secure. You are responsible for device security (passcode, OS updates) and for safeguarding backup files you export.

Children

The App is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your region). Do not use the App if you are under that age.

International users

Your information may be processed in the country where you use the device and in data centers used by Google/Firebase. By using the App, you understand that data may cross borders where those providers operate.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated. The “Last updated” date will change when it does. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the revised policy.

Contact

Questions about this policy: cwizard2011@gmail.com.
